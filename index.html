Agros Ice Cream

Hello, there!

We're glad you found us. Our name is Agros and we have a passion for ice cream--now before you write us off as another Baskin Robbins, hear us out. We're different. We're organic. By golly, we're healthy! How is that possible you might ask? Well, our ice cream is made with vegetables (stop cringing), fruit and milk substitutes like rice, coconut, and almond milk. But somehow, we managed to make it taste so delicious we actually prefer it to "normal" ice cream. So jump on in, to the new world of ice cream--we promise you won't regret it.
